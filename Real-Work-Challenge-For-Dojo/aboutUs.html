About Us


At WalkUp, we're revolutionising the way people eat out. We help over 400,000 diners find seats at over 800 restaurants every month – making us one of the UK's most popular restaurant queue management systems.

We launched in 2018 with an idea to make booking restaurants super-fast and hassle-free through the tap of an app – and have seated over 20 million diners since. That's just the beginning. We’ve recently embarked on a new chapter with payments provider Dojo, which helps thousands of pubs, restaurants, and other hospitality businesses take payments fast, securely and conveniently.

That means today, WalkUp diners have access to tables at even more awesome restaurants across the UK – and a world where any diner can grab a table anywhere, anytime is within reach.
